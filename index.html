About PROMEDI TRANSPORT

Learn about our journey, values, and commitment to providing exceptional logistics solutions across South Africa.

Our Commitment to Excellence

Founded in 2025, PROMEDI TRANSPORT SERVICES has rapidly established itself as a trusted provider of comprehensive logistics solutions in South Africa. Our journey began with a single truck and a vision to transform the transportation industry through reliability, innovation, and exceptional service.

Today, we operate a modern fleet of vehicles and employ a team of highly skilled professionals dedicated to ensuring your goods reach their destination safely and on time. Our strategic location in Johannesburg allows us to serve clients across Gauteng and beyond efficiently.

We pride ourselves on our compliance with all regulatory requirements, holding official registration with the Companies and Intellectual Property Commission (CIPC), which underscores our commitment to operating with integrity and transparency.
